About


Thank you for visiting our website! Boo is a home-grown organization that strives to help others enjoy fall as much as we do. It all started when my neighbors kept calling me for my favorite fall recipies and techniques on how to make amazing pumpkins. I just knew there was something to this and to share. Fall is a time of change, of helping others, of growing and embracing all that nature has to give us. Enjoy some of my favorite recipies and decorating tips and share the good news. How did it go? What more could have been done? We look forward to seeing your glowing pumpkins and hear of the delectible treats you make.
