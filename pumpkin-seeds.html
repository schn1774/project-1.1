Homemade Pumpkin Seed Recipe


   Pumpkins are good for pretty decorations, and amazing pumpkin foods, but the seeds? It only gets better. This recipe is a simple and easy classic way to make a crunchy, crispy snack. No longer will the pumpkin seeds get thrown away or only be used for next year's crop.

   For more variety in your snack, change up the seasonings with paprika and chili powder or cinnamon and sugar or whatever fall vibe you are going for!




INGREDIENTS

1 Small Pumpkin

1 Tsp Salt

1 Tbsp Olive oil

MATERIALS

Baking Sheet

Parchment Paper

Carving Knife

Spoon

Large Bowl

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1:

  Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farenheight and cover baking sheet in parchment paper.

Step 2:

  Cut off top of pumpkin and scoop out guts and seeds. Soak seeds and guts in cold water and use hand to separate pumpkin guts from all seeds. Remove from water and place on paper towel. Pat dry.

Step 3:

  Place dried seeds in bowl and add olive oil and Salt. Spread out on prepared baking sheet in single layer.

Step 4:

  Place in oven for 10-15 minutes or until crispy and remove from oven. Let cool for best flavor.
